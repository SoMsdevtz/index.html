Welcome to Our School Website

Peace and Love Schools are private schools that are registered by the Tanzania Ministry of Education. The schools consist of nursery, primary, and secondary levels, and provide education for both girls and boys.

Peace and Love Nursery and Primary School is a day school located at Ubungo near Mawasiliano road. The school aims to provide quality education in a conducive environment that supports students in their academic and spiritual growth.

PRE UNIT SCHOOL

"Welcome to our exceptional school where education begins at a young age. Our pre-unit level is designed to provide a supportive and engaging environment for young learners. Our teachers are committed to helping each child reach their full potential and fostering their growth and development.


PRIMARY SCHOOL

Primary school continues to build on this strong foundation, offering a curriculum that emphasizes hands-on learning, critical thinking, and a love of learning. Our students are well-prepared to face the challenges of secondary education and beyond. We invite you to come and see the difference a quality education can make in your child's life. Enroll now and give your child the gift of a bright future."


PRE, PRIMARY & SECONDARY IN KILIMANJARO

Primary and Secondary school located in Kilimanjaro provide conducive learning environment for students away from the hustle and bustle of city life .We have a clear vision and commitment to combine academic excellence education following Tanzania national circullum with well rounded development in spiritual,social and intellectual process.
