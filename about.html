About Peace and Love Primary School

Peace and Love Primary School was founded in 2010 with just 10 students. Today, the school has grown to serve over 500 students of all genders, from baby class to standard 7. As a Christian school, Peace and Love strives to instill a love for God in its students and to provide education and service to students of all religions.

School Motto

"The fear of the Lord is the beginning of wisdom" - These words embody the school's commitment to educating students with a foundation of wisdom and a desire to serve others.

School Mission

The mission of Peace and Love School is "to educate men and women for others". With this goal in mind, the school works to prepare students to become men and women who serve others, with a sense of love and responsibility for the common good. Inspired by the Christian tradition, the school strives to instill a spirit of service in its students.

School Vision

Peace and Love School aims to achieve excellence and to educate students to become men and women for others. The school's vision is to help students reach their full potential, with a focus on recognizing and developing individual gifts and talents. With the help of parents, teachers, and fellow students, the school strives to provide a comprehensive education that prepares students to work towards the greater good of their society and the glory of God.
